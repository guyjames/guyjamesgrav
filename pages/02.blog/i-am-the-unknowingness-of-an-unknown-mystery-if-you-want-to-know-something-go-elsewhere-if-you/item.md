“

I am the unknowingness  
 of an unknown mystery.  
 If you want to know something,  
 go elsewhere.  
 If you want to un-know everything,  
 then sit and listen.

The silence inside of you  
 is the sound of your knowledge collapsing.  
 Remember, it is you who said,  
 “I want to be free.”

”

 – **

Adyashanti
